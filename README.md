## 🚀 Featured Innovations & Projects Spotlight

### 🎰 1. Sports Betting Platform – Soroban Smart Contract
> **Category:** Web3 / Decentralized Finance • **Hackathon:** Stellar Buildathon 🏆
- **Overview:** Engineered a non-custodial decentralized sports betting platform on the **Stellar Network** leveraging the **Soroban SDK (Rust)**. Eliminates central bookmakers and guarantees trustless payout execution.
- **Architectural Highlights:**
  - Designed transparent, immutable on-chain data structures to store user match selections, stake pools, and odd multipliers.
  - Developed multi-sig admin verification for match outcome declaration with fail-safe rollback logic.
  - Implemented gas-optimized reward claim smart functions allowing bettors to withdraw winnings directly to their Stellar wallets.
  - Deployed and verified end-to-end operational flow on the **Stellar Testnet**.
- **Tech Stack:** `Rust` • `Soroban SDK` • `Stellar Blockchain` • `Smart Contracts`

---

### 🎨 2. Sanskriti-Flow – Immersive E-Commerce Platform
> **Category:** Full-Stack Web / Social Impact • **Hackathon:** InnovateX (MAKAUT) 🌟
- **Overview:** Conceptualized and built a hyper-local e-commerce ecosystem directly linking traditional Bengali artisans (weavers, potters) with global buyers, eliminating manipulative middleman supply chains.
- **Architectural Highlights:**
  - Formulated an **"Immersive Storytelling"** product discovery engine, allowing consumers to experience the rich heritage, crafting process, and bio of each artisan before making a purchase.
  - Architected a direct-to-artisan revenue distribution model supporting rural micro-entrepreneurs.
  - Pitched the full platform architecture and economic rationale at **InnovateX MAKAUT** to acclaim from industry judges.
- **Tech Stack:** `Full-Stack Web` • `TypeScript` • `Node.js` • `UI/UX Design`
